Privacy Policy — GNIS

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

GNIS ("Reverse Karaoke") is a party game app developed by Masami Kawamoto. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

GNIS does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account or registration is required to use the app.

Microphone Access

GNIS requests access to your device's microphone solely to enable the reverse karaoke gameplay. Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

In-App Purchases

GNIS offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock the full version. All payments are processed securely by Apple App Store. No financial data is stored by GNIS.

Children's Privacy

GNIS does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: gnis.support@gmail.com
